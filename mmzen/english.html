About me

My name is Mariana Martinez. I am from Venezuela and crossed the border when I was 20. I have been living in Belgium for 17 years now that I can call my home. My passion is to meet people, listen to their stories and help them. For me, a massage is a way to provide help and care to others to relieve them of their stress, emotional blockages and muscle pain. I enjoy giving massage and am happy to see my clients relaxed. I have obtained several diplomas in my field. So ... choose a massage that suits you and will help you feel better. Preferably stop or switch off your mobile phone during the treatment.

If you want to reschedule or cancel an appointment, I would like to hear this at least 24 hours. in advance.
